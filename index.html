INTRODUCTION

Capital Academy is the first organized academy in pasrur.it was established when there was scarce any opportunity especially for the students of FSc in 2006. meeting the dire needs of that time and place,capital academy kept on hiring new trends and teaching methodologies time to time.
we are proud to pronounce that many of our students are working as teachers/lecturers at different schools, colleges and academies not only in pasrur region but also far off areas.
we always tried our best to provide the proper career counselling to our students and always encourage them so that they can participate in the building of our society. it is esteemed to tell our present dear students that our trained students are successfully working in approximately every segment of society as e.g. engineers,doctors, pharmacists, computer scientists, lecturers, teachers, fine arts specialist, fashion designers, journalists, and managers. capital academy is serving in the fields of science subjects meeting the needs of students for their ultimate goal; success in exams. only experienced and learned people can help meet this challenge and this academy has succeeded in this perspective. along with the context of instruction, academy serves "character building", "mind making", "grooming" and "mending individuals" to make them better products for enhancing sophisticated social living and standards. currently,
our priority and plan is to help our students being able to paas out entrance examinations for universities. many efficient and brilliant students find it hard to pass those exams. we plan to prepare them at academy beforehand by giving them opportunity to solve that level of papers with proper standardize methodology.
