  • Capital History

Four pillars of Capital Academy; Sir Majid Rafique , Sir Mudasar Hussain , Sir Ehsaan Ilahi Zaheer and Sir Atif Shaheen built this organization. Step by step the success graph went high from faculty members 4 to 18 today. Since 2006 Capital Academy has been offering complete preparation and guidance for FA, FSc, I. Com, BA, BSc, B. Com, MA English, MA Urdu, MA Islamic Studies, MBA and now BS level. Talking about the Quality of Study, firstly our students are the best embodiment of Quality, secondly; billboards, flexes, pamphlets and handbills all over the city approve the Quality Sign that come out on every result declaration.
